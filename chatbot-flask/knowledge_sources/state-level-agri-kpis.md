# State-Level Agriculture KPI Framework

## Strategic KPI families
- Production volume and yield trend.
- Input access and affordability.
- Climate risk and resilience indicators.
- Market stability and food supply continuity.

## Reporting standards
- Monthly regional aggregation.
- Confidence annotation for each KPI source.
- Explicit data quality flags for missing values.

## Decision workflow
- Detect low-performing regions.
- Assign intervention priorities by severity.
- Track policy impact over rolling quarters.

## Transparency
- Publish summary dashboards for public stakeholders.
