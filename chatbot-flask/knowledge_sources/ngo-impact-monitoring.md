# NGO Impact Monitoring

## Program tracking
- Define beneficiaries, outcomes, and timeline per project.
- Track baseline vs current values by zone.
- Record constraints and mitigation actions.

## Field coordination
- Sync NGO activities with cooperative schedules.
- Avoid overlap of interventions in same time slot.
- Share risk alerts with public partners.

## Reporting package
- Monthly narrative summary.
- Quantitative KPI appendix.
- Geo-segmented impact highlights.
