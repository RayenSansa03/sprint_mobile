# Pest and Disease Alert Playbook

## Monitoring cadence
- Inspect representative plots at least twice per week.
- Record visible symptoms, spread pattern, and weather context.
- Use threshold-based action instead of reactive spraying.

## Preventive strategy
- Maintain spacing and airflow to reduce fungal pressure.
- Remove infected residues quickly.
- Rotate active ingredients to avoid resistance.

## Escalation criteria
- Fast spread over multiple plots.
- Repeated outbreak despite treatment.
- Significant expected yield impact.

## Technical note
- Link alert severity with intervention priority in planning dashboard.
