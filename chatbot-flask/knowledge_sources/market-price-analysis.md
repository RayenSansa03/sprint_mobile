# Market Price Analysis Guide

## Weekly analysis workflow
- Collect prices by product and by region.
- Compute weekly average, min, and max values.
- Compare against previous week and seasonal baseline.
- Detect unusual spikes and identify potential causes.

## Decision support for producers
- Delay sale when trend is strongly rising and storage is safe.
- Prioritize quick sale when trend is sharply declining.
- Diversify buyers to limit counterparty risk.
- Pair market trend with weather and logistics context.

## Cooperative use case
- Publish a weekly bulletin for members.
- Highlight product categories with high volatility.
- Recommend contract or spot strategy by category.
