# Weather Risk Response

## High heat
- Shift irrigation windows to early morning.
- Increase canopy protection and mulching.
- Watch crop stress indicators around midday.

## Heavy rainfall
- Improve drainage pathways before peak events.
- Delay nitrogen applications to avoid losses.
- Inspect roots and disease pressure after rain.

## Wind events
- Secure supports and vulnerable structures.
- Delay pesticide applications under high wind.

## Planning integration
- Pair short-term forecasts with weekly task reprioritization.
- Mark weather-dependent tasks as conditional.
