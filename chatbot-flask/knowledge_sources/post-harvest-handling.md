# Post-Harvest Handling

## Immediate handling
- Harvest at proper maturity stage.
- Avoid direct sun exposure after picking.
- Sort and remove damaged products quickly.

## Quality and value preservation
- Use clean crates and gentle stacking.
- Keep products in cool and ventilated space.
- Minimize time between harvest and market dispatch.

## Traceability
- Log lot ID, harvest date, and producer.
- Attach destination channel and sale outcome.

## KPI examples
- Loss rate by product category.
- Average delay from harvest to sale.
