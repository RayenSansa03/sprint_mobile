# Cooperative Operational Guidelines

## Weekly governance cycle
- Monday: member demand and issue collection.
- Wednesday: planning and market alignment review.
- Friday: execution review and KPI summary.

## Member coordination
- Standardize reporting templates for field status.
- Group procurement for critical inputs.
- Centralize logistics booking for transport efficiency.

## Performance indicators
- Member participation rate.
- On-time task completion.
- Average gross margin by product line.

## Risk management
- Maintain fallback supplier list.
- Publish incident communication protocol.
