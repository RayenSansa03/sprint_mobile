# Irrigation Best Practices

## Scheduling by growth stage
- Seedling stage: short and frequent irrigation to keep topsoil moist.
- Vegetative stage: increase volume, reduce frequency to encourage deeper roots.
- Fruiting stage: maintain stable moisture to avoid stress and cracking.

## Practical field rules
- Irrigate early morning to reduce evaporation losses.
- Prefer drip lines for water efficiency and disease reduction.
- Trigger irrigation using soil moisture sensors when available.
- Avoid overwatering near harvest to preserve quality.

## Tomato-specific guidance
- Typical summer target: keep moisture stable in root zone.
- Watch for blossom end rot linked to uneven watering.
- Use mulch to reduce daily water fluctuations.
